Outcomes

Strategic – 5-7 Years

  • Economically Feasible access to the Lunar surface for scientific exploration, commercial development and settlement.
  • Permanent human settlement beyond Low Earth Orbit.
  • Infrastructure sufficient to support exploration missions to the Asteroid Belt, Mars, and further destinations. This crewed infrastructure includes communications, fuel depot, robotic support and nodes to connect additional modules for expanded capabilities.
  • Technical knowledge, legal capacity and regulatory authority to design and construct the LSEI.
  • Full funding to complete LSEI construction.
  • Team established to design LSEI hardware
  • Complete understanding of international policy, regulatory, legal, environmental and military requirements to construct and launch the LSEI
  • Funding to complete initial design, feasibility and basic operations
  • LSEI Design Study
  • LSEI Feasibility Study
  • Financing for Year 1, 2, and 3
  • Board of Advisors Conference(s)
  • StringSat Experiment (1 of 3) Built and Launched
